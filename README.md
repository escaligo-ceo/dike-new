# Dike.cloud

> **"Your profession in order, your time in balance."**

Dike.cloud is an intelligent legal management platform designed to restore balance to the modern lawyer's life. Developed under the **Escaligo** umbrella, it transforms deadline management from a source of stress into an automated, secure, and seamless process.

## Vision
Built as a **digital fortress**, Dike.cloud integrates:
* **Smart Scheduling:** Dynamic deadline management with "Hot/Cold" logic.
* **Data Lifecycle Management:** Optimized storage and high-tier data protection.
* **Audit & Analytics:** Granular tracking for maximum transparency and security.
